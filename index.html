Your Retreat

A Home Perched
Above the Water

Fjordhjem sits 300 feet above Hood Canal's glacial waters, offering unobstructed views of the Olympic Mountains to the west and the serene fjord below. This isn't just a cabin; it's a front-row seat to nature's daily performance.

Watch bald eagles soar from your deck. Listen to the tide shift below. Track weather systems rolling across the mountains. Here, the boundary between inside and outside blurs into something extraordinary.

The Experience

Life at the Edge
of Wilderness

Harvest Your Dinner

Walk down to Twanoh Falls State Park at low tide and harvest fresh oysters straight from the canal. There's nothing quite like oysters you pulled from the water yourself, shucked on the beach, with mountain views as your backdrop.

Paddle the Fjord

Launch from the nearby marina and explore Hood Canal's calm waters. Early morning paddles reveal glassy reflections of the mountains. Evening sessions deliver sunsets that paint the entire sky.

Watch the Wildlife

Bald eagles nest nearby and hunt along the shoreline. During salmon runs, watch nature's cycle play out from your deck. Seals, herons, and osprey are regular visitors. Bring binoculars.

Follow the Salmon

Hike to Twanoh Falls during spawning season and witness thousands of salmon making their journey upstream. It's one of the most powerful natural spectacles in the Pacific Northwest.

Location

Perfectly Positioned
in Hood Canal

Fjordhjem sits in the sweet spot between accessibility and seclusion. You're close enough to civilization for groceries and great meals, but far enough to feel worlds away.

Twanoh Falls State Park

Right down the road. Beach access, oyster harvesting, salmon viewing, and hiking trails through old-growth forest.

Belfair (10 min north)

Safeway, QFC, Grocery Outlet for supplies. Local restaurants, bars, and small-town amenities when you need them.

Union & Alderbrook (10 min south)

Alderbrook Resort welcomes everyone to their waterfront property. Upscale dining, cocktails, spa services, and stunning grounds worth exploring.

Olympic National Park

Less than an hour's drive to rainforests, alpine lakes, and mountain trails. Hurricane Ridge, Lake Cushman, and Staircase are all day-trip accessible.
